Welcome to NAJ LIBRARY

Objective

Create a comprehensive and user-friendly sports library website that provides resources for athletes, coaches, sports researchers, and fans.

Goals

  • Provide access to sports resources (books, journals, databases, articles).
  • Enable athlete performance tracking and data-driven insights.
  • Integrate sports news and event schedules.
  • Facilitate research on sports science, coaching, fitness, and more.

Target Audience

  • Athletes (high school, college, and professional).
  • Coaches and sports trainers.
  • Sports researchers and academics.
  • Fans and enthusiasts of specific sports.

Technology Stack

Decide the tools, platforms, and technologies to use (WordPress, Drupal, or custom-built platforms).

Strategy

  • Sports-related research articles.
  • Athlete profiles with performance data.
  • Training resources and guides.
  • News and event updates in the sports world.
