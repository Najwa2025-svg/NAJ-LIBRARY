Arif Aiman

⚽ Football Player – Malaysia

Arif Aiman is one of Malaysia’s brightest young football talents. Known for his incredible pace and dribbling skills, he has become a key player for both his club and national team. He plays primarily as a winger and is admired for his technical ability and tactical awareness on the pitch.
