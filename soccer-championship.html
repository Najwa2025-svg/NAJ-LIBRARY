Local Soccer Team Wins Championship

The Tigers have done it! On May 30, 2025, they emerged victorious in the regional championship, defeating Rivals FC in an electrifying match. With a perfect combination of teamwork, agility, and precision, they brought honor to our community.

  • Final score: Tigers 3 - 1 Rivals FC
  • Top player: Amir Hakim (2 goals)
  • Venue: National Arena, Selangor
